The Decision

We needed a public face. We used our own tools.

After completing the HTML-as-first-class-citizen work and the full GAN mechanization, the next natural step was obvious: dogfood the harness by shipping a real, beautiful public site.

We chose only Rust-based tools (Zola for marketing, mdBook for documentation) to stay consistent with our technology preferences and to demonstrate that the harness works for non-Rust deliverables as well.
