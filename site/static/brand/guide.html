Brand Overview

CrossR Skills is a curated collection of high-quality Agent Skills for AI coding agents (Claude, Cursor, Codex, etc.). Built for functionally obsessed developers who demand Rust-level excellence: functional purity, layered architecture, zero technical debt, and self-verifying code.

Every skill is crafted with the same reverence as worship — excellence as an act of faith (Colossians 3:23). The hammer represents forging and craftsmanship. The Cross is our foundation. The flames symbolize resurrection power and refining fire.
